// DEV LOG

Follow the journey of Funkie Football development

GAMEPLAY Dec 15, 2024

New Trick System Implemented

Added a brand new skill move system! Players can now perform rainbow flicks, elasticos, and the signature "Funkie Flip" that sends goalkeepers flying. Still tweaking the timing windows.

VISUALS Dec 10, 2024

Stadium Lighting Overhaul

Completely reworked the stadium lighting system. Night games now have that electric atmosphere with dynamic crowd shadows and volumetric light beams. Xande outdid himself on this one!

MULTIPLAYER Dec 5, 2024

Online Matches Beta

First successful online match! Latency compensation is working better than expected. Shaun managed to score a hat-trick against Xande from across the country. Bragging rights secured.

AUDIO Nov 28, 2024

Crowd Chants & Commentary

Recorded custom crowd chants and implemented dynamic commentary that reacts to gameplay. The "GOOOOOAL!" scream is absolutely perfect. Can't stop scoring just to hear it.

MILESTONE Nov 20, 2024

Core Gameplay Complete!

Major milestone achieved! All core gameplay mechanics are now implemented. Passing, shooting, tackling, and goalkeeping all feel tight and responsive. Time to polish and add the funkie flair!
